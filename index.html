About

I am a second year PhD student in Computer Science at the Stony Brook University and a member of CVLab, co-advised by Prof. Michael Ryoo and Prof. Dimitris Samaras. My broad research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning. Currently, I am exploring better representations for video understanding and video synthesis.
Prior to this, I worked as a Research Assistant at University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, under the supervision of Dr. Ranga Rodrigo. I completed my B.Sc. in 2018 specializing in Electronic & Telecommunication Engineering at University of Moratuwa, advised by Prof. Dileeka Dias and Dr. Prathapasinghe Dharmawansa.
